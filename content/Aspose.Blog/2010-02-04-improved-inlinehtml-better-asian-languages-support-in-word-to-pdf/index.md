One of the most demanding features by our potential customers is to convert HTML pages into PDF format. InlineHTML is a method that can be used to transform HTML page into PDF format and in a new HotFix of Aspose.Pdf for .NET, a quite stable version of InlineHTML feature is provided. Besides this, earlier some of the customers have faced issues while converting multilingual Word document into PDF format. In this version, the support for languages other than English is greatly improved. In short, this HotFix includes fixes for most of the defects reported recently and is a quite stable version.

In order to view a complete list of new features and fixes and to download HotFix for Aspose.Pdf for .NET 4.1.2, please visit [this link][1].
