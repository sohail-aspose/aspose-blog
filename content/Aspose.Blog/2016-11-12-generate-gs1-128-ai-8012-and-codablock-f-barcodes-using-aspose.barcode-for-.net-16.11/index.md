## Enhancements

Following is a list of improvements included in this release.

*   Classification of the encode types (symbologies) has been incorporated
*   Process of reading QR barcode from multi-page Tiff has been improved.
*   Process of datamatrix barcode recognition has been improved.
*   Exception handling process while generating PDF417 barcode has been improved.
*   Functionality to generate PDF417 barcode has been improved.
*   Process of reading MicroQr barcode has been improved.

To view a complete list of new features and fixes and to download the latest release, please visit [Aspose.Barcode for .NET 16.11 page in downloads section][4].
